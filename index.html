AUDIT ANALYSIS

The Structural Void: 8-Grid Chaos

03 Critical Leak

01. THE PROBLEM: Choice-to-Nowhere Loop

🔴 Barrier Type: Information Overload + Fake Doors

The current site presents 8 distinct service icons (Air, Sea, Rail, Road, Customs, etc.). However, all 8 paths lead to the same generic /services page. The user's specific intent is ignored.

Result: High cognitive load (F) + Low certainty (D) = Booking abandonment

02. THE LEAK: Diagnosis Deficit

B = M × (D + F) × P
PROBLEM: Low Diagnosis (D) + High Friction (F) = Zero Booking (B)

The prospect must self-diagnose their freight type before the system can help them. This burden shifts the cognitive work from the sales team (where it belongs) to the prospect (where it kills conversion).

03. THE WALL OF CHOICE

Asset: Wall of 8 ChoicesStatus: Cognitive Overload

Prospects are overwhelmed before they've even selected a service. This is where most bookings die.

04. THE FRICTION: Data-Entry Black Box

Asset: Form FrictionStatus: 10+ Data Points Required

Even after choosing a service, prospects are forced to fill 10+ fields without a 'certainty signal' in return (e.g., "Your lane is covered by our WCA-certified network"). This is primary D+F failure.

05. THE SOLUTION: Freight Certainty Triage (FCT)

Proposed Asset: FCT BlueprintStatus: Ready for Implementation

Replace the 8-grid with a 3-step diagnostic that moves the burden from the prospect to the system.

STEP 1
Intent Triage: Urgency Selection
Replace 8 choices with 3 intent-based buttons:
  • (A) Time-Critical → Air Freight Specialist
  • (B) High-Volume → Sea/Rail Bulk Shipper
  • (C) Specialized → Project/Dangerous Cargo Expert
STEP 2
Destination Locking: Network Validation
Single-field input: "Where are you shipping to?"

Instantly triggers a P-Signal: "✓ WCA Certified Network Active in [Country]"

This provides certainty that their freight type + destination is feasible.

STEP 3
Frictionless Handover: One-Field Quote
Email-only field (system already knows cargo type & destination)

Button text changes from "Submit Form" to:

🔒 SECURE SHIPMENT REQUEST → [SLA & WCA Logos]

Trust anchors transform a form submission into a secure action.
